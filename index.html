◆ About This Fundraiser



This fundraiser was conceived by Eliza Becker, a Sophomore at Croton-Harmon High School and member of the Varsity Soccer, Ski, and Lacrosse teams. In addition to generating Tiger spirit in the stands, this project seeks to raise funds for two causes she is passionate about: Croton-Harmon Athletics and the health of the Hudson River. By sourcing the stadium seats and handling all customization through her own labor, these high-quality seats are offered at a competitive price while generating proceeds for the CHHS Booster Club and Riverkeeper.



All proceeds from the sale of stadium seats goes toward supporting the following two not for profit organizations: the Croton-Harmon Booster Club, which helps support the athletic programs and extracurricular activities at Croton-Harmon High School, and the Riverkeeper, which brings together science, law, and advocacy to heal the Hudson River and safeguard our drinking water.
